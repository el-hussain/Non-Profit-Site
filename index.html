Light of Youth Initiative for Transparency and Accountability

Light of Youth Initiative for Transparency and Accountability is a youth-led, non-governmental organization based in Borno State, Nigeria, with a vision to expand across Northern Nigeria.

We are committed to promoting good governance, transparency, and accountability by empowering young people with the knowledge, skills, and platforms to actively participate in civic and community development processes.

Learn about our project

Who We Are

Our Vision

A society where young people actively shape governance processes and where transparency, accountability, and good governance are upheld at all levels.

Our Mission

To empower young people through civic education, capacity building, and community engagement to promote transparency, accountability, and active participation in governance.

Values

  • Transparency and Accountability
  • Good Governance
  • Youth Empowerment
  • Inclusion and Equity
  • Community Engagement
  • Integrity and Leadership
